Support

Need help with Cosmic Pop Galaxy? Contact us by email:

cosmicpopgalaxy@gmail.com

We typically respond within a few business days. When contacting us, please include:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • App version (shown at the bottom of in-app Settings)
  • A short description of the issue

Common topics: gameplay questions, progress saved on your device, optional rewarded videos, lives and Stardust, audio settings.

Privacy Policy

Cosmic Pop Galaxy does not collect, store, or share personal information. All game progress (levels, lives, Stardust, boosters) is saved only on your device using local storage.

Optional rewarded videos (Google AdMob): On iOS and Android you may choose to watch a short video to earn in-game Stardust. Ads load from Google’s servers when you tap “Watch video.” We request non-personalized ads only. Daily limits (up to 10 views per calendar day) are enforced on your device. Google may process device and ad data per Google’s Privacy Policy. We do not use other analytics, accounts, or cloud sync.

Core gameplay works offline. Rewarded videos require an internet connection.

Audio: Background music and sound effects play locally on your device. You can mute music and sound effects in the in-app Settings screen.

Privacy contact: For privacy questions, email cosmicpopgalaxy@gmail.com.

Changes: We may update this page. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
