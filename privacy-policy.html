Cosmic Pop Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Cosmic Pop does not collect, store, or share personal information. All game progress (levels, lives, Stardust, boosters) is saved only on your device using local storage.

We do not use analytics, advertising, accounts, or cloud sync in this version of the app.

The app works offline and does not require an internet connection to play.

Audio: Background music and sound effects play locally on your device. You can mute music and sound effects in the in-app Settings screen.

Contact: For support or privacy questions, use the support link on the App Store listing for Cosmic Pop.

Changes: We may update this policy. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
